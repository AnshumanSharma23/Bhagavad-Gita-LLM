27. All actions are wrought in all cases by the qualities of Nature only. He whose mind is
deluded by egoism thinks: “I am the doer”.
COMMENTARY: Prakriti or Nature is that state in which the three Gunas exist in a state
of equilibrium. When this equilibrium is disturbed, creation begins and the body, senses and mind
are formed. The man who is deluded by egoism identifies the Self with the body, mind, the
