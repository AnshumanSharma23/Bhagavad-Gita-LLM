Thus, this most secret science has been taught by Me, O sinless one! On knowing this, a
man becomes wise, and all his duties are accomplished, O Arjuna!
Hari Om Tat Sat
Iti Srimad Bhagavadgeetaasoopanishatsu Brahmavidyaayaam
Yogashaastre Sri Krishnaarjunasamvaade
Purushottamayogo Naama Panchadasho’dhyaayah

This discourse is important and very instructive to all persons who wish to attain happiness,
